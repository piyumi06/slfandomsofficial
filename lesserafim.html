🌸 LE SSERAFIM

LE SSERAFIM is a South Korean girl group under SOURCE MUSIC,
a label under HYBE. Debuting in 2022, the group is known for
their confident concepts, powerful performances, and unique
musical style. Their official fandom name is FEARNOT, representing
the strong connection between LE SSERAFIM and their fans.
