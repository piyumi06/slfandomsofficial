Frequently Asked Questions

Is membership free?

Yes. Community Membership is completely free.

Can I join more than one fandom?

Absolutely! You can participate in as many fandom communities as you like.

Do I have to be from Sri Lanka?

SLFO primarily supports Sri Lankan fandoms, but international fans are welcome to participate where applicable.

Will there be paid membership?

Yes. A Supporter Membership with additional benefits is planned for the future.

Do I need an account?

Member accounts, login, and digital membership cards will be introduced in a future update.
