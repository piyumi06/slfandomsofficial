💚 NCT

NCT (Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy group under SM Entertainment.
Known for its unique concept of multiple sub-units and unlimited members,
NCT currently promotes through units including NCT 127, NCT DREAM, WayV,
and NCT WISH. With diverse music styles and outstanding performances,
NCT has built a passionate global fanbase known as NCTzen (Czennie).
