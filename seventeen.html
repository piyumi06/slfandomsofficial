Seventeen

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean K-pop boy group known for their powerful performances,
self-produced music, and exceptional teamwork. Debuting in 2015 under Pledis Entertainment,
the group consists of 13 members divided into the Hip-Hop, Vocal, and Performance units. With a dedicated global
fanbase called CARAT, SEVENTEEN has gained worldwide recognition for their creativity, synchronized choreography,
and diverse musical style.
