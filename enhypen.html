🧡 ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN is a seven-member South Korean boy group under BELIFT LAB.
The members are Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki.
Known for their powerful performances, captivating concepts, and diverse music,
ENHYPEN has built a passionate global fanbase known as ENGENE.
