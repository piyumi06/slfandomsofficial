💙 PERSES

PERSES is a five-member Thai boy group under G'NEST.
The members are Jung, Plugg, Palm, Krit, and Nay.
Known for their powerful performances, impressive vocals,
and modern music style, PERSES has earned a dedicated fanbase
in Thailand and around the world.
