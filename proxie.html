🌙 PROXIE

PROXIE is a six-member Thai boy group under bROTHERS MUSIC.
The members are Gun, Kim, Chokun, Gorn, Onglee, and Victor.
Known for their energetic performances, strong vocals, and
engaging personalities, PROXIE has built a growing fanbase
across Thailand and internationally.
