🌌 EXO

EXO is a South Korean boy group under SM Entertainment.
The group consists of Suho, Xiumin, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen,
Chanyeol, D.O., Kai, and Sehun.
Known for their outstanding vocals, powerful performances, and diverse music,
EXO has become one of the most influential K-pop groups with a global fanbase known as EXO-L.
