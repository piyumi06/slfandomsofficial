🧡 RIIZE

RIIZE is a six-member South Korean boy group under SM Entertainment.
The members are Shotaro, Eunseok, Sungchan, Wonbin, Sohee, and Anton.
Known for their fresh concepts, outstanding performances, and diverse music,
RIIZE continues to gain fans around the world with their unique style and talent.
Their official fandom name is BRIIZE.
