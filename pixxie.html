💜 PiXXiE

PiXXiE is a three-member Thai girl group under LIT Entertainment.
The members are Mabel, Pimma, and Ingkho. Known for their
catchy songs, charming performances, and unique style,
PiXXiE has become one of Thailand's most popular girl groups
with a growing international fanbase.
