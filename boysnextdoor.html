🚪 BOYNEXTDOOR

BOYNEXTDOOR is a South Korean boy group under KOZ Entertainment,
a label under HYBE. Debuting in 2023, the group is known for
their relatable music, fresh concepts, and natural charm.
Their official fandom name is ONEDOOR, representing the special
connection between the members and their fans.
