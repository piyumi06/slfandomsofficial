🌟 About Our Team

SL Fandoms is a growing organization created to bring together different fandom communities across Sri Lanka. Our team works to connect fans, support communities, and create a welcoming space where everyone can discover and enjoy their favorite fandoms.

👑 Founder & President

Leads the organization, manages the overall vision, and guides future development of SL Fandoms.

⭐ Vice President

Supports the founder, coordinates projects, and helps manage organizational activities.

📢 Community Managers

Manages fandom connections, supports fan communities, and maintains communication between members.

🎨 Creative & Media Team

Handles content creation, designs, promotions, and social media activities.

💻 Website & Technology Team

Maintains the website, improves digital platforms, and manages technical development.
