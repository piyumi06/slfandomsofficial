✨ ATLAS

ATLAS is a seven-member Thai boy group under XOXO Entertainment.
The members are Erwin, Jet, Nice, Muon, Tad, Junior, and Poom.
Known for their powerful performances, impressive vocals, and
versatile music, ATLAS has gained a growing fanbase in Thailand
and internationally.
