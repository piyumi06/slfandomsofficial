🌸 4EVE

4EVE is a seven-member Thai girl group under XOXO Entertainment.
The members are Mind, Jorin, Taaom, Hannah, Fai, Punch, and Aheye.
Known for their outstanding vocals, energetic performances,
and versatile music, 4EVE has become one of Thailand's leading
girl groups with fans across the world.
