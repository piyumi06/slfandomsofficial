🐺 LYKN

LYKN is a five-member Thai boy group under RISER MUSIC (GMM Grammy). The members are William, Lego, Nut, Hong, and Tui. Since their debut, LYKN has gained fans worldwide through their energetic performances, powerful vocals, and versatile music.
